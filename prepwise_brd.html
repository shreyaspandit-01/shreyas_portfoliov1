The Problem Worth Solving

Every serious PM and consulting candidate faces the same preparation problem. Interview prep tools exist — but none of them simulate the actual experience of being in a room with a Zepto PM or a McKinsey partner. They give you question banks, frameworks, and generic feedback. They don't give you the real thing.

The tools that do exist are built around two outdated assumptions: that preparation and hiring are separate processes, and that all interviewers want the same thing. Neither is true. Zepto's PM interviews are nothing like CRED's. A McKinsey case is nothing like a BCG one. The rubrics are different, the personas are different, the expectations are different.

The core gap: No platform combines company-specific depth, AI-powered simulation, cross-session skill tracking, and real feedback calibrated to an actual company's bar — for the two highest-demand career tracks in India.

Why Now

Three forces are converging. First, PM roles have multiplied — every funded startup, every enterprise company building digital products needs product managers, and the bar for entry has risen with supply. Second, Claude and modern LLM APIs have made it possible to build company-specific, persona-driven simulations that weren't feasible two years ago. Third, India's candidate pool is deeply underserved by Western tools that have no understanding of how Zepto or Razorpay actually interview.

The India wedge: PrepWise's deepest advantage is India-specific company depth. No global tool has 9+ Indian company rubrics, personas, and case libraries built from ground up. This is the moat that gets built session by session — and it's already live.

The Dual Revenue Bet

PrepWise operates on two sides simultaneously. Candidates pay to prepare. Companies, eventually, pay to access pre-scored, rubric-matched candidate pipelines. Every candidate who uses PrepWise is also creating a performance record that has B2B value — a data asset no competitor is building.

03

What PrepWise Does

PrepWise is a live AI interview platform. A candidate picks their track, picks a target company, and enters a simulated interview with an AI architect that behaves like that company's actual interviewer — same rubric, same probing style, same expectations. After the session, they get feedback calibrated to that company's bar, not a generic score.

The session follows a structured four-moment arc: a briefing before the interview starts, a warm opening from the architect, the main case with real-time probing, and a closing where the candidate asks questions. Every session ends with a resolved interview — not an infinite loop of follow-ups.

What makes it different: The Readiness Score is cross-session. Every session updates a running model of the candidate's skill across six dimensions. The longer they use PrepWise, the smarter the model of them becomes. Switching to a competitor means starting from zero.

04

Tracks and Scope

PrepWise V1 focuses on two tracks — PM and Consulting. These are the two highest-demand, highest-willingness-to-pay career tracks in India with the most structured interview formats. Additional tracks are on the roadmap as the core product matures.

Scope decision: Business & Operations and AI tracks are intentionally excluded from V1 and V2. The PM and Consulting tracks are where the depth, the rubrics, and the company personas are strongest. Shipping two tracks with real depth beats shipping four tracks with shallow coverage.

PM Track — Live

Covers Product Manager, APM Programs, AI PM, and Product Designer roles. Nine Indian companies live with full rubrics and personas: Zepto, CRED, Razorpay, Meesho, PhonePe, Groww, Flipkart, RentoMoko, and PhysicsWallah. Case types span product sense, metrics, execution, prioritisation, and strategy.

Consulting Track — V2

Covers MBB (McKinsey, BCG, Bain), Big 4 Strategy, and In-house Strategy teams at Indian companies. McKinsey uses interviewer-led cases with PEI deep-dives. BCG and Bain are candidate-led. Case types include profitability, market entry, M&A, pricing, and growth. The four-moment session arc maps directly to how real consulting interviews are structured.

User Personas

Primary · PM Track
The Final-Year Student
Engineering or MBA student targeting APM roles at Indian startups. Has theoretical PM knowledge from courses and books. Needs company-specific simulation to close the gap between knowing frameworks and actually applying them under pressure. Will pay ₹199–299/month without hesitation if the product feels real.
Primary · Consulting Track
The Case Prep Grinder
IIM or top college student targeting MBB or consulting roles. Already knows the frameworks. What they need is reps — high-quality case reps with feedback that tells them whether their answer would pass at McKinsey specifically, not just generically. Practices daily for 2–3 months before placement season.
Secondary
The Career Switcher
2–4 years of experience in engineering, consulting, or operations. Targeting a PM role for the first time. Has strong domain knowledge but no PM interview experience. Uses PrepWise to build confidence and understand how their experience maps to PM interview expectations.
Secondary
The Active Job Seeker
Has a real application in front of them — an interview scheduled in 2 weeks. Uses Target Mode, not Practice Mode. Needs a session built specifically around that company's JD and their own resume. Highest conversion from free to paid of any user type.
06

Revenue Model

B2C — Candidate Subscriptions

Monthly subscription at ₹199–299/month gives access to all tracks, unlimited sessions, full Readiness Score tracking, and the session transcript with annotated feedback. Free tier gives 3 sessions to establish habit before the paywall. The pricing is intentionally low — below the cost of a single human mock interview session.

B2B — Talent Pipeline (V4+)

Companies pay to access pre-scored, rubric-matched candidate pipelines. A startup looking to hire a PM can filter for candidates with a Readiness Score above 7.5 against their specific rubric — and get a shortlist of PrepWise users who've already been evaluated on exactly what they care about. This is the two-sided marketplace endgame. No competitor is building toward this.

The B2B insight: Every B2C session creates a data asset. PrepWise earns on both the preparation side and the hiring side — a position no existing prep platform occupies. The B2C subscription funds product development. The B2B pipeline is the high-margin outcome.
07

Competitive Positioning

PrepWise's positioning isn't incremental — it's categorical. The comparison isn't "better than Exponent." It's "the only platform that does all of this together": company-specific depth, AI simulation, cross-session intelligence, and an eventual B2B pipeline.

The positioning line: Deeper than Exponent, more AI-native than Interview Kickstart, more India-specific than any global tool, and building toward a B2B layer that no pure prep platform has attempted.

Moat Strategy

Depth compounds. Every company rubric built, every case prompt added, every session stored makes PrepWise harder to copy. A competitor starting today would need months to replicate the India company library alone — and they'd be doing it without the session data that makes the Readiness Score meaningful. The switching cost grows with every session a user completes.

Versioning & Roadmap

PrepWise ships in four versions, each building on the last. No timelines — versions ship when the core of each phase is solid, not by calendar.

V1
Core Engine Live
Full PM track live. 9 Indian company personas with rubrics and case libraries. JD + resume input triggering real-time personalised sessions. Complete interview loop from setup through feedback. Deployed on Vercel.
V2
Guided Setup + Consulting Track
Redesigned onboarding flow with session intent, architect introduction, and goal-setting. Consulting track launch with MBB personas. Four-moment session arc with proper case resolution. Live dimension tracker and post-session transcript replay.
V3
Intelligence Layer
Resume DNA engine — CV parsing and background-calibrated case generation. Full Readiness Score with cross-session comparison. Blind spot detection. Next session brief auto-generated before every session. Voice mode with filler word detection and pacing analysis.
V4
Platform + B2B Layer
Global leaderboard. Jobs marketplace with "Practice for this role" button. B2B talent pipeline — companies access pre-scored candidate pools filtered by rubric match. Peer mock matching. Elite Club for top performers. Global scale with no hardcoded templates.
